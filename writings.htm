ANIKA TABASSUM N.P. TITHEE

okay, I want to introduce myself as a rhymer though I also like to write articles. In my writings of articles and stories, I want to reveal myself as protesting and in this regard I couldn't proceed as far as I wanted. I fell down because of my distress about some matters of my life. but gradually I'm over coming my position.
I'll express my writings here any time. now I want to write a rhythmic greeting from me. hope you'll enjoy it.

"well wishes will be yours,
good luck will be mine,
please receive my salaam,
that's all about for thine."
